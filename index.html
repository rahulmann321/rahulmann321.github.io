High Protien Meal Plan

Breakfast

- 1 Banana

- 200 grams of Protein Yoghurt

- Rolled Oats

- 1.5 cups of Unsweetened Oat Milk

Combine the Rolled Oats with the Oat Milk, heat for 2 mins, and add sliced banana on top. Enjoy the yoghurt as a side.

Lunch

- 135 grams of Frozen Veg

- 250 grams of Precooked Rice

- 225 grams of Chicken Breast

Chop your Chicken into bite sized chunks. On medium high heat, fry your chicken until coooked, whilst also boiling your Frozen Veg until soft.

Dinner

- 135 grams of Frozen Veg

- 200 grams of Beef Mince

- 133 grams of Sweet Potato

Snacks

- 2 Green Apples

- 0.5 cups of Mixed Berries

- 1 Peach

- 1 Cappuccino

Enjoy Snacks throughout the day.

Nutrition
